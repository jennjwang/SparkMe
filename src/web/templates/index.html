Welcome to the AI Interviewer

Thank you for participating in our research study. Each week, the AI Interviewer will conduct a short check-in about your recent work — what tasks you worked on, roughly how much time each took, which tools you used, and who you collaborated with.

Your first session is a longer intake (20–30 minutes) that establishes your baseline work profile. Subsequent weekly check-ins take around 10 minutes. The interviewer remembers what you've shared in prior sessions and asks targeted follow-ups, so the conversation becomes more specific over time.

How it works: You can respond using text or voice. The conversation is open-ended — describe your work in your own words. There are no right or wrong answers.

Privacy Notice

You are not required to share any personally identifiable information. We do not collect or store PII.

You do not need to provide:

  • Full name, age, or date of birth
  • Physical address or precise location
  • Contact information (phone, email)
  • Government IDs or financial information
